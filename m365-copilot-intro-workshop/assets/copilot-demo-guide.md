# Facilitator Demo Guide — Intro to M365 Copilot (60 minutes)

A lightweight run sheet for delivering the live demos. Keep each demo to its
time box; the goal is momentum and "aha" moments, not completeness.

| # | Segment | Time | What to show |
|---|---------|------|--------------|
| 1 | Welcome & what Copilot is | 5 min | Where Copilot lives across M365; grounding in your work data |
| 2 | Copilot Chat | 10 min | "Catch me up" + find a file + draft a status update |
| 3 | Word | 10 min | Draft a brief, summarize, rewrite a section |
| 4 | Outlook | 10 min | Summarize a thread, draft a reply, coaching |
| 5 | Teams | 8 min | Meeting recap + action items |
| 6 | Excel & PowerPoint | 12 min | Analyze a table; generate a short deck |
| 7 | Prompting tips & wrap-up | 5 min | Prompt formula, refine vs. restart, Q&A |

## Setup checklist (before class)
- Sign in with a Copilot-licensed Microsoft 365 account.
- Have a sample Word doc, an email thread, a Teams meeting recap, and a small Excel table ready.
- Close sensitive content; use demo or synthetic data only.
- Test your network and that the Copilot icon appears in each app.

## Demo tips
- Narrate your prompt as you type it so learners see the structure.
- Show one *refinement* per demo ("make it shorter", "more formal").
- Pause for a "your turn" prompt in at least two segments.
- Reinforce the privacy message: Copilot respects existing permissions.

## Common questions to be ready for
- "Does it use my company data to train the model?" — No, your data stays within your tenant's compliance boundary.
- "Can other people see my prompts?" — No, your Copilot interactions are private to you.
- "Why did it get something wrong?" — Copilot can make mistakes; always review outputs before sending or sharing.
