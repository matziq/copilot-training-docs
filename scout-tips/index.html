Microsoft Scout — Tips & Tricks

Get more out of your AI desktop assistant. Click any card to expand.

🚀 Getting Started

🎯 Describe the outcome, not the steps ›

Scout chooses its own tools. Tell it the goal and let it act.

  • ✅ "Find every email from finance this week and summarize the action items."
  • 🚫 "Open Outlook, click search, type finance…"
💬 It works from Teams too ›

Message Scout in Microsoft Teams and it can run tasks remotely, then reply with the result. Great for kicking off a job from your phone.

🛑 Steering mid-task ›

You can interrupt and redirect at any time. A quick "stop", "no", or "actually do X instead" reshapes the current task — it won't sign you out or kill services.

📧 Email & Calendar

🔍 Smart inbox triage ›

Scout can list, search, read, reply, forward, move, and draft email.

  • "What unread mail needs a reply today?"
  • "Draft a reply to Sarah's budget email — keep it short."
  • "Forward the contract from legal to my manager."
📅 Scheduling that respects OOF ›

Ask it to find meeting times across people and it checks availability, Out-of-Office, and tentative blocks before proposing slots.

"Find 30 min with Alex and Priya next week and send the invite."

✋ Drafts before sends ›

Have it save as draft when you want to review first. Scout also previews recipients and content before sending anything visible to others.

💬 Teams & People

📨 Send & reply in chats ›

"Message Jordan on Teams that I'll be 10 minutes late." Scout resolves the right person by email so it lands in the correct chat — including @mentions.

🧭 Org navigation ›
  • "Who is my skip-level manager?"
  • "Find the PM who owns the onboarding project."
  • "Who do I work with most on the data team?"

📁 Files & Documents

📂 It can reach outside the workspace ›

Point Scout at a file in Downloads or Documents by full path — it'll ask once to grant the folder, then work with it. Don't assume a file is off-limits.

📊 Real Office documents ›

Scout builds genuine .docx, .xlsx, and .pptx files — TOCs, formulas, charts, speaker notes — not just plain text.

"Turn this CSV into a formatted Excel report with a pivot chart."

☁️ OneDrive & SharePoint ›

Browse, download, and upload OneDrive/SharePoint files — including co-authoring-safe uploads back to a shared doc URL.

🌐 Web & Browser

🕹️ Real browser automation ›

Scout drives a real browser: navigate, fill forms, click, extract data. Useful for portals without an API — expense systems, booking sites, internal tools.

🔎 Live web search ›

It pulls real-time info from the internet and fetches specific pages, so answers reflect today — not stale training data.

⚙️ Automations & Memory

⏰ Scheduled automations ›

Set recurring jobs that run on their own.

  • "Every weekday at 8am, summarize my unread mail and Teams."
  • "Each Monday, check for better flight seats on my reservation."
🔔 Condition triggers ›

Beyond schedules, Scout can watch for a condition and act when it happens — e.g. "tell me when an email from the CFO arrives."

🧠 It remembers preferences ›

Tell it how you like things done ("always keep RSVP comments generic", "open links in my Work profile") and it stores that for next time. Ask it to forget anytime.

🛠️ Skills specialized

⚡ Prebuilt workflows ›

Skills are deep, tuned workflows invoked with a slash command. A sampling:

  • /expense-report — file Dynamics 365 expenses from receipts
  • /business-travel-booking — book Microsoft travel
  • /convert-to-html5 — turn a deck or doc into a web page
  • /web-artifacts-builder — interactive dashboards & visuals
  • /lifecycle-report — Patch Tuesday status workbook
🎨 Documents & visuals on demand ›

/pptx, /docx, /xlsx, and /excalidraw produce polished decks, documents, spreadsheets, and diagrams from a short prompt.

⭐ Power Moves

🔗 Chain across services in one ask ›

The real magic is combining tools: "Pull the action items from yesterday's project meeting, draft a follow-up email to attendees, and put a summary doc in my OneDrive."

🔒 Privacy by default ›

Your mail, files, calendar, and chats are private. Scout won't leak schedule or personal details into outbound messages and keeps RSVP comments generic.

🎚️ Make it yours ›

Adjust personality, switch the underlying AI model, toggle dark mode, and tune settings — all by just asking.
