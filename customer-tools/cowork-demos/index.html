Trainer Tips

Swap names if needed

Prompts use demo names like Alex Johnson and Contoso. Replace with real tenant users for more personalized demos.

Starred prompts are demo-ready

Prompts marked ⭐ DEMO PROMPT are tested, high-impact, and reliably produce impressive output. Lead with these.

Cancel before confirming

For scheduling and sending demos, Cowork shows a preview before taking action. Decline at the confirmation step to avoid live sends during demos.

Iterate live

After any output, show iteration: "Make it shorter." or "Add a note about the budget." This demonstrates Cowork's conversational memory.

Test your connection first

Verify Cowork access the morning of the training. Have screenshots of expected output ready as a backup in case of connectivity issues.

Use real data

Your actual inbox and calendar make demos far more compelling than fabricated examples. Participants see real, personalized results instantly.
